Users Choice

User-Friendly Interface Gmail’s design is intuitive, which means it's easy to navigate even for someone new to email. You can easily find your inbox, compose emails, and manage settings without needing a steep learning curve.Free Storage Gmail offers 15GB of free storage shared between Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Photos. This large space allows you to store emails, attachments, and files without worrying about quickly running out of storage.

Security

Gmail is very good at identifying unwanted or dangerous emails and sending them directly to the Spam folder. This keeps your inbox clean and helps protect you from phishing attacks or malicious content.Gmail is used by billions of people, which speaks to its reliability and trustworthiness. It’s been around for a long time and continually improves, which makes people confident in choosing Gmail as their primary email service.
